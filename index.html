Welcome to Maldives

The Tropical Paradise of the Indian Ocean

Maldives is famous for crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, luxury resorts, white-sand beaches, islands, lagoons, sunsets, marine life, snorkeling, relaxation, paradise.

Maldives offers overwater villas, turquoise seas, tropical islands, palm trees, calm lagoons, colorful fish, diving spots, romantic sunsets, peaceful atmosphere.

Mas Huni

Traditional Maldivian breakfast made with shredded tuna, coconut, onion, chili, lime, usually eaten with roshi.

Garudhiya

Clear tuna soup flavored with salt, curry leaves, onions, chilies, served hot with rice and lime.

Fihunu Mas

Grilled fish marinated in Maldivian spices, chili, garlic, and lime, popular beachside seafood dish.

Rihaakuru

Thick fermented tuna paste used as flavoring, spread, or curry base in Maldivian cuisine.
